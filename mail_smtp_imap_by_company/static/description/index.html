Email Aliases with Multiple Domain and Multi Company

Geminate comes with a feature to support multiple domain and multi company emailing system with multi company incoming and outgoing mail server.







Editions Supported

1. Enterprise Version

2. Commnuity Version

Available Key Features

  • Companywise Incoming and Outgoing Email Server.
  • Multiple alias domain configuration based on company.
  • Email alias can have multiple domain selection.
  • You can configure N number of email aliases with multiple domains and every email will fetch in the exact destination model as per configuration.
  • No limit of single website domain based email aliases.
  • User can exactly represent the company under which they are working in terms of multi company environment as you can configure multiple outgoing mail server and it will automatically select based on active company of logged in user. no email spoofing.
  • At the time of fetching emails, it will look for associated company and 'Create a Record' will belongs to that company only so easily manage records between multiple companies created through incoming mail server.
